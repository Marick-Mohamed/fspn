About the firm

Overview

FSPN Is a full-service law firm that constitutes of several advocates, legal consultants and a number of paralegal and administrative staff. The firm was established on September 2018. The outboasting and fast-growing firm has two founding members who are Fatma Said Ahmed Kimaro and Pendoveera Hussein Nyanza.
The firm currently has its offices at around municipality of Kinondoni, Plot No. 21 Kohler Building, 2nd Floor along Ali Hassan Mwinyi Road, Dar es Salaam. With its branch office located in KATAVI region at MPANDA municipality at around Police road Street.
