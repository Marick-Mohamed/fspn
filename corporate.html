Corporate Law

FSPN CRB’s corporate and commercial practice incorporates at least six companies a month. Mainly on behalf of multinational companies seeking to set up a corporate presence in Tanzania. These clients are mostly in the sectors of mining, banking, insurance, beverages, insurance, real estate and agriculture. All the partners at the firm have vast experience and insight in both local and cross-border merger and acquisition transactions

Our service include
  • Processing of the relevant tax files (for Value Added Certificate and Tax Identification Number Certificate)
  • Processing work and residence permits.
  • Company secretarial services
  • Company Incorporation
  • Corporate Governance and Board Structuring
  • Corporate Restructuring and Re-organisations
  • Obtaining Regulator Licences and Permits
  • Competition Advisories
  • Transfer of Shares
  • Sale of a Corporation’s Assets
  • Advice and Set up of Group Company Structures
